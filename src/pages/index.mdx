# Next Generation Cartographers

Our group aims at creating _the_ safe space for the next generation of scientists, professionals and enthusiasts in the field of cartography.
A space to share work, find inspiration and connect with fellows.

## Activities

Information about _NGC_ related events, presentations, etc.
